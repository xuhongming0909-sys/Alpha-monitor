**Architecture Guardrails**:
- Build `数据抓取/`, `策略中心/`, `页面展示/`, and `消息推送/` before moving business logic.
- Use plugin-style folders under `数据抓取/` and `策略中心/`; each business domain owns one folder.
- Plugins may depend only on `共享基础/*`, `共享基础/总线/*`, and files inside their own plugin folder.
- All fetch outputs must be normalized onto The Bus before strategy consumption.
- Changing notification style must stay inside `消息推送/` and must not require edits in other domains.

## Format

- `[ID] [P?] [Story] Description`
- `[P]` means parallel-safe work with disjoint write scope
- `[US1]` unified monitoring and push
- `[US2]` market rules and strategy behavior
- `[US3]` config vs runtime-state separation
